About ProjX

SEQ's Appointed Project Marketer. Not a Middleman.

ProjX is the developer's exclusive, appointed project marketer across 18 active estates in South East Queensland. When you enquire through this portal, you're talking directly to the team that sits at the source — the same people who set the price, manage the stock, and control the release schedule.
